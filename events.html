Some examples where using solar powered batteries has worked very nicely.



Easter Sunrise service at Castle Island in South Boston
3/31/2013, 4/20/2014


2013 was the first year not using a traditional generator to power the event and it was very nice hearing the sounds of Boston Harbor in the background during the service.

Taking less time to set up and not running 100' of power cord and 30' of Yellow Jacket was also nice ...



Wincester Town Day
6/3/2012, 6/1/2013, 6/7/2014


Powering the small sound system on the Town Common with solar powered batteries solved the problem of running 100' of power cord through a very crowded public space to get to the closest electrical outlet.



Covenant School Jog-a-thon
5/11/2013, 5/5/10/2014


Yes ... It works in the rain.



Boston Pops Brass Ensemble, Franklin Park, Boston
6/24/2012


Classical music in the beautiful Frederic Olmstead park setting with no generator running in the background!

Having the client ask "When are you going to turn it on?" was a real eye opener ...
It was on!
