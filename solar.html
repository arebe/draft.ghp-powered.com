Why use solar power for your next event?



For starters ... there is no noise! With no generator running in the background you will be able to hear everything ... Not just the loud parts.

With no moving parts, exhaust fumes or flammable liquids involved, solar power is a wonderfully safe and reliable way to power an event.

No long cable runs ... No cable ramps ... Just clean, quiet dependable power.

My solar powered systems use solar panels to charge deep cycle batteries.

Quick and easy to set up ... The batteries are brought to the stage, a switch turned on and ... Power!

The solar panels stay "home" ... Letting me bring power almost anywhere.

I currently have three different sized systems available.
Perfect for a public address system at your info booth, charging your cell phones and warlike talkies ... Even a play!

Perfect for a classical, folk or jazz concert ... Even an entertainment stage at a street Faire!

Perfect for outdoor concerts with an audience of up to 1,000 people ... Or how about block party /dance party?


Used individually or together to power larger, longer events, solar charged batteries are a great way to power your next event.
