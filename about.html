Making sense!

Solar power ... A neat idea ... But does using it to power your event make sense?

Maybe!

Every event is different.

There are lots of things to consider when planning an event ... How you will power it is only one of them.

There are always the financial aspects to consider. Site logistics, amount of set up /tear down time available, anticipated power requirements, environmental and safety concerns and artistic vision will all need to be figured out.

Your power source will be chosen after considering some, if not all of these things ...

You may be surprised at the number of times you will want to ... and be able to ... "go solar"!
