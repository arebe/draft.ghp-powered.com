GHP Powered is my new project specializing in silent portable power.



I am Greg Hanawalt and I have an amazing way to power your next outdoor event ... Solar charged batteries!

Until now, finding power for an outdoor event has not always been easy ... and often the source has been less than ideal. If you were lucky, a long cable run was all that was required ... If you were not ... There was a noisy generator at the other end of that long cable run.

As owner of Strand Sound I have been setting up sound and lighting systems for a wide range of events since 1982.

In 2012 I started powering some of those events using solar charged batteries.

No noise, no fumes and no long cable runs ...

Just quiet, clean, reliable power ... Wherever, whenever you want it!
